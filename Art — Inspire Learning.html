Art

Grade Levels: K-5

Course Description

This virtual art course introduces young students (K-5) to the joy of creative expression, all from the comfort of their homes. Through Zoom, students engage in hands-on art activities that explore drawing, painting, sculpting, and mixed media. Each session encourages imaginative thinking and provides step-by-step guidance to help students develop their unique artistic voices. Lessons are interactive, allowing for live feedback, collaboration with classmates, and sharing of artwork in a supportive online space. The curriculum is designed to inspire creativity while helping students build foundational art skills and confidence.

Key Skills Developed: 

Creative Expression: Encourages students to explore personal ideas and emotions through various art forms.

Fine Motor Skills: Develops hand-eye coordination and precision with art tools.

Visual Perception & Observation: Enhances awareness of shapes, colors, patterns, and textures.

Art Vocabulary & Communication: Teaches students to describe their art process and engage in discussions about their work.

Problem Solving & Patience: Builds resilience by encouraging students to experiment, learn from mistakes, and adapt their work.

Self-Confidence & Reflection: Promotes self-expression and personal growth through sharing and discussing artwork.

Materials

A recommended materials list will be provided for each grade level, focusing on accessible items like colored pencils, crayons, watercolor paints, markers, clay, and basic household supplies for crafting.
